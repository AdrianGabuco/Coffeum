Our Story

Welcome to Coffeum, your destination for exceptional coffee and warm hospitality. Founded in 2020, Coffeum began as a passion project by Adrian Gabuco and Erica Jane Vasquez, a dedicated coffee enthusiast with a vision to create a haven for coffee lovers. What started as a humble corner café has evolved into a beloved establishment that brings people together over the love of exceptional coffee.

Values

At Coffeum, our values are rooted in authenticity, community, and craftsmanship. We believe in fostering connections, inspiring creativity, and delivering an unforgettable experience to every guest who walks through our doors.

Commitment to Quality

Our commitment to quality is unwavering. We source the finest beans from around the world, working directly with farmers who share our passion for excellence and ethical practices. Every batch of coffee is roasted with precision to preserve the distinct flavors that make each origin unique. Our baristas are artisans, meticulously trained to craft each cup with precision and care. From the moment the beans are ground to the final pour, every step is a celebration of the art and science behind a perfect cup of coffee. We also take pride in our dedication to sustainability. From our eco-friendly packaging to our partnerships with local suppliers, we aim to make a positive impact on the environment and the communities we serve.

Creating Moments

Coffeum isn't just about coffee; it's about creating moments. Whether you're seeking a peaceful retreat to catch up on work, a spot to connect with friends, or simply a place to enjoy the simple pleasures of life, our café is your space. As we continue to evolve, our commitment to delivering exceptional coffee and a warm, welcoming atmosphere remains steadfast. We invite you to join us in celebrating the journey from bean to cup, one sip at a time. Indulge. Connect. Brew your moments.

Our Mission

At Coffeum, our mission is to provide a haven for coffee enthusiasts where each cup is a journey, and every sip is an exploration of flavor. We are dedicated to sourcing the finest beans from around the world, carefully roasted to perfection by our expert team. Our commitment to excellence extends to creating a warm and inviting atmosphere, making every visit a delightful experience. With a blend of passion, quality, and community, we strive to create a coffee culture that connects people, stimulates conversations, and fosters memorable moments. Join us in celebrating the art of coffee and the joy it brings to our lives.
